How to Set-Up your Device

Set-Up requires the use of a smartphone--if you do not own or regularly operate a smartphone your emergency contact or family members can carry out these easy-to-follow steps in your home.


User Setup Video



Step 1) Set-Up your Weight Scale

For this step you will need:

  • A computer
  • Nokia Body-BMI Wi-Fi Scale
  • A smartphone

1A) On your smartphone, download the Withings Health Mate app on your smartphone.

(Go to the app store. Search for "Withings Health Mate App" in the search bar. Download the Withings Health Mate App)

1B) Sign-in to your account with the login details provided for Withings Health Mate

1C) Click the Devices menu at the bottom right corner and then click on Install a device

1D) Click on Scales and then choose the Body scale. Follow the next instructions in the Withings Health Mate app to connect your Nokia Body-BMI Wi-Fi Scale.

*Please note: This scale is meant for one user only. Others stepping onto your scale, especially if they are close to your weight, may interfere with this device’s functioning.


Step 2) Set-Up your Light

For this step you will need:

  • A lamp
  • The LIFX Mini Color light bulb
  • A smartphone

2A) Connect your light bulb to a lamp in your home. This lamp must remain turned on 24/7 and should be in a room where you spend the most amount of time daily.

2B) Turn on the power of the lamp and turn the switch for the lightbulb on and off 5 times to activate it. Then turn the light on for the next steps.

2C) Download the LIFX app onto your smartphone

2D) Sign in on the app with the login details provided for the LIFX app

2E) Click the + sign in the top right hand corner to connect your light

2F) Click on the LIFX Set Up button and follow the steps in the app to connect the light to Wi-Fi.

2G) Once this has been set up, rename the light bulb with the name provided.


This light serves as a Reminder and Alert System

Reminder System

Your light will turn BLUE every day at 3 PM to remind you to step onto the scale. It will change back to regular white light when your have done this.

*Please note:This color-change process will take up to two minutes to occur.

To set up a different reminder time please follow these instructions:

Go to ifttt.com and click My Applets. From there click on the applet that says “Every day at 3 pm turn light ON”. This applet will have a settings button on the top right corner which you can click, then scroll down to change the reminder time setting.

ALERT System

YOUR LIGHT WILL TURN RED IF YOU HAVE GAINED AN UNHEALTHY AMOUNT OF WEIGHT.

Please proceed to make an appointment with a physician. At your doctor’s appointment, you should discuss that your weight has increased abnormally in recent weeks.
